ABOUT

The BYU Mechatronics Club aims to help fill the need of a multi-disciplinary education that is needed in industry today. Our organization helps to empower students from a variety of backgrounds by providing them with a broad range of engineering and technical skills not offered by any single department; including an understanding of mechanical design, microcontrollers, programming, modeling, prototyping, etc.
All are welcome to attend—come build cool things with us.
